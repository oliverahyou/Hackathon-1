Weight Gain


How to know if you need to do a weight gain diet?

The standard answer is that your body mass index (BMI) is a measure of your body fat. It uses your weight, height, and age to give you a healthy range. In general, if your BMI is less than 18.5, you're underweight.

To find your BMI, use a BMI calculator. A BMI of:

  • 18.5 or less is considered underweight
  • 18.5-24.9 is considered ideal weight
  • 25-29.9 is considered overweight
  • 30 or higher is considered obese
