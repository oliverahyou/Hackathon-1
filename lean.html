Lean


What is a lean meal?

"Get lean" refers to decreasing body fat.With a lower body fat percentage you will be "leaner" than if you have a high body fat percentage. It's the balance between muscle mass and fat mass in your body.

The process is to shred some fat from you body without losing much muscle mass. Someone with a lean body will look thin but equally toned up and healthy.


Breakfast

  • 3-4 egg whites + 1 yolk cooked
  • 1/2 cup strawberries or any fruit of choice

Mid Morning Snack

Mid Afternoon Snack
